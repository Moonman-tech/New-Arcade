How it works

We have projects aimed at promoting small and large scale farming, educating people further about farming, planning agricultural fairs and sponsoring technological advances in the agricultural sector. Through donors, our projects can be completed and the community we are looking to create will grow much bigger.

Projects

Donors will be invited to our exclusive donor community where they can help us make better spending choices. Being one of our major donors means being a part of our administration as you help us achieve our goals. Major donors are a part of our board and they are as important to the company as board members.

Donors

Being a donor does not necessarily mean donating money, a farmer can donate a piece of their land for our small projects. Water and farming equipment could also be regarded as currency in some situations. A donation of such is welcomed and we will try to do the best with what was donated to achieve our company goals

Farmers

Those looking to learn about agricuture can easily do so and people are now more aware of free and open market. The presence of the youth in farms is increasing overtime and the agricultural sector is slowly growing and people are starting to realise that the agricultural sector is very profitable and open.
